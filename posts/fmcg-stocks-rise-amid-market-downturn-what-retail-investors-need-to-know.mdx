This article aims to explore the dynamics behind this trend, offering insights into why these stocks are currently gaining traction and what it means for investors looking to diversify their portfolios.

FMCG stocks are considered defensive plays in the stock market, often delivering consistent returns even when the overall market is bearish. This characteristic stems from the essential nature of the products offered by the FMCG sector, such as food, beverages, and household items.
