## Detailed Analysis
### The CLARITY Act
The CLARITY Act aims to provide a clear legal definition of cryptocurrencies, distinguishing them from other financial instruments. By establishing a consistent regulatory framework, the Act seeks to eliminate the ambiguity that has long plagued the crypto industry.

### The GENIUS Act
The GENIUS Act focuses on fostering innovation within the crypto space by providing tax incentives for blockchain-related research and development. This legislation is designed to encourage technological advancements and position the US as a leader in blockchain innovation.

### Anti-CBDC Surveillance State Act
This Act seeks to limit government surveillance capabilities associated with Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDCs). By imposing restrictions on data collection and privacy infringements, the Act aims to protect individual freedoms while embracing digital currency advancements.
