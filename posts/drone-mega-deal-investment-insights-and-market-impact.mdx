## Market Context

The defense sector has been experiencing a steady increase in demand driven by geopolitical tensions and technological advancements. According to the Stockholm International Peace Research Institute (SIPRI), global military expenditure reached nearly $2 trillion in 2022, with significant investments in drone technology. This trend is expected to continue, with countries seeking to enhance their military capabilities and maintain strategic advantages.

The drone industry, in particular, has seen rapid growth due to its applications in surveillance, reconnaissance, and combat operations. The global drone market is projected to reach $58.4 billion by 2026, growing at a CAGR of 13.8% from 2021. This growth is fueled by advancements in AI, machine learning, and autonomous systems, which enhance the operational efficiency and effectiveness of drones.
